NES Retro Multiplayer

Host runs the emulator, renders the game, and streams the canvas over WebRTC. Guest sends only input over a PeerJS data channel. No ROMs are uploaded anywhere.

Host (Player 1)

Run the emulator, load a ROM from your machine, and receive Player 2 input.

Guest (Player 2)

Use keyboard, touch, or a controller as the second player. No emulator needed.

Open Guest
